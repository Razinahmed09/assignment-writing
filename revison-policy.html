Revision Policy

Our Commitment to Satisfaction

At Assignment Writing Services, our primary goal is to ensure customer satisfaction. If you are not fully satisfied with our work, we offer unlimited free revisions to meet your specific requirements. Simply contact our customer service team, which is available 24/7, to request a revision.

Providing Clear Instructions

When requesting a revision, please provide detailed instructions that align with your original requirements. If the new instructions do not match the initial requirements, we may not proceed with the revision.

Additional Charges for New Requirements

If your revision request includes new or different requirements from the original instructions, additional charges may apply. Our free revision service is intended to meet the original requirements you provided.

Impact on Delivery Date

Please note that if you request a revision close to your deadline, the delivery date may change. We strive to accommodate your needs, but revisions may require additional time.

Revision Request Period

After receiving your completed assignment, please review it thoroughly. You have two weeks to inform us if you are satisfied or if you require any revisions. This allows us to make any necessary adjustments promptly
